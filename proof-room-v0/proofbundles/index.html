ProofBundle

ProofBundle Example

A ProofBundle is the record of how the work was done.

What it answers

  • What was the mission?
  • Which agents worked?
  • What did they produce?
  • How was it checked?
  • What became reusable?
  • What improved for the next cycle?

Why it matters

In ordinary AI use, the output can disappear into the workflow. In an AI-First enterprise, the work becomes evidence, memory, and capability.
