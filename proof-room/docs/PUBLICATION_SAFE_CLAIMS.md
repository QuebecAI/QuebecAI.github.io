# Publication-safe claims

Use:

- Public evidence layer.
- Flagship Evidence Docket.
- ProofBundle example.
- Capability Archive initialized.
- Demonstration of the Proof Room format.

Avoid claiming:

- Achieved AGI or ASI.
- Empirical SOTA.
- Autonomous production deployment.
- External certification.
- Guaranteed economic return.

Stronger claims require live tasks, baselines, replay logs, safety/cost ledgers, external reviewers, delayed outcomes, and independent reproduction.
