# Publication-safe claims

Use these claims publicly:

- The Proof Room is a public evidence layer.
- ED-001 demonstrates the Evidence Docket format.
- The Proof Room shows how enterprise work can become governed, verified, reusable AI-First capability.
- The Capability Archive preserves reusable assets for stronger future cycles.

Do not claim:

- External benchmark superiority.
- Client results without client approval.
- Confidential enterprise deployment.
- Autonomous production remediation.
- Achieved AGI or ASI.
